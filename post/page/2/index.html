Masters subject

I found another couple of interesting images from my masters. They represent cycles orthogonal to a single cycle. A cycle is a representation of all numbers equidistant from a complex or double number (numbers of the form x + yj, where j squared is one). Here are double cycles orthogonal to the x axis.
